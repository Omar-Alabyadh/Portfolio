    Weather App

    Welcome to the Weather App, a responsive web application that provides real-time weather information using an API. With the Weather App, you can easily access weather data for your current location or any city of your choice, Responsive For All Screens.

    Get Weather by Device Location :- With the click of a button, the Weather App can automatically detect your device's location and display the current temperature, "Feels Like" degree, and humidity level. Simply press the "Get Device Location" button, and the app will instantly retrieve the weather data specific to your location. Stay informed about the current weather conditions wherever you are.

    City name :- Or simply enter the name of the city whose weather conditions you want to know.
